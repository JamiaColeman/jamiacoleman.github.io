My Career Goals

My goal is to build a long term career in data analytics where I can use technology and data to solve real business problems. I want to continue improving my skills in SQL, Excel, Power BI, reporting, dashboards, and data storytelling.

In the short term, I want to gain a role where I can strengthen my experience with business reporting, data validation, and dashboard development. In the long term, I hope to grow into a role that allows me to lead projects, improve processes, and support strategic decisions through data.

According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, employment for data scientists is projected to grow much faster than average, which shows strong opportunity in data focused careers (U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, 2025). I also find it encouraging that operations research analyst roles are projected to grow much faster than average as well (U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, 2025).
