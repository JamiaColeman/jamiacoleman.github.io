Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to express my interest in a data analyst or operations reporting role with your company. I have developed strong experience working with data, reporting, and workflow support in fast paced environments. Through my background in operations, hospitality, and logistics, I have learned how to organize information, solve problems, and communicate clearly with different teams.

In my recent work, I used Excel, SQL, and reporting tools to track information, review patterns, and support daily decision making. I enjoy turning raw information into useful insights that help teams improve performance. I am especially interested in roles where I can use data to support efficiency, quality, and business growth.

One of my strengths is my ability to stay organized while managing large amounts of information. I also bring a strong work ethic, attention to detail, and a willingness to keep learning. I believe these skills would allow me to contribute positively to your team from day one.

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my experience and goals align with your organization. Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,
